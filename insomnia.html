Frequently Asked Questions

What happens in the free assessment?

I take a full medical history and ask about your current sleep habits. I'll share evidence-based sleep tips and assess whether you'd be a good fit for the full program — it's a chance for us both to decide if this is the right approach for you.

How is the program delivered?

The entire eight-week program is delivered virtually. You can join from wherever you are — no travel, no clinic waiting rooms.

How does the eight-week structure work?

Each week builds on the last. We start with a thorough assessment and blood testing, then work through structured sessions that progressively address the behavioural, lifestyle, and clinical factors affecting your sleep.

Will it work?

I can't guarantee individual outcomes — sleep is complex and personal. But the principles I use, including CBT-i, are effective in around 80% of people based on large meta-analyses. That's why I focus on evidence-based methods rather than quick fixes.

Can I do this if I have other medical conditions?

Absolutely — many people with other health conditions benefit from better sleep. We do a full assessment to see if the program is the right fit for both of us before you commit to anything.

Is this a regulated service?

Yes. Regulated activities are provided via ThanksDoc, which is registered with the CQC.
