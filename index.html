The Cost of Burnout and Poor Health

  • UK employees were off sick on average 9.4 days per employee in 2024 (CIPD)
  • Poor workplace health costs UK employers £85 billion annually (Gov.uk)
  • 21% of people needed time off work due to stress according to the Mental Health UK Burnout Report 2025 (Mental Health UK)
  • Reduced productivity, staff retention and engagement scores (Great Place to Work)

How Dr Sandhu Can Help

We deliver in-person and virtual wellbeing experiences for the office to reduce burnout risk, promote engagement and optimise health for your employees. These aren't your usual corporate talks though ...

Genuinely Entertaining

Dr Sandhu is a competition-winning spoken word artist. He has performed at TEDx and even No.10 Downing Street. Littered with on the ground stories training as a doctor, he will keep this engaging and super interactive. Delivery is everything.

8 Years as a Medical Doctor

As a fully qualified GP working in the NHS and Private Sector, Dr Sandhu has years of experience working with professionals with work-related stress, burnout and many other mental and physical health conditions. He has further training in Lifestyle Medicine.

Lived Experience

From pursuing academic and sporting excellence at one of the world's top Universities to long junior doctor night shifts and literally saving lives on the roadside, Dr Sandhu knows pressure. He also knows what it's like to experience burnout, recover from it and thrive in high-stress environments.

Personalised to Your Workplace
