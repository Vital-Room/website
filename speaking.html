Speaking Services

Genuinely Entertaining

Dr Sandhu is a competition-winning spoken word artist. He has performed at TEDx and even No.10 Downing Street. Littered with on-the-ground stories from training as a doctor, he keeps sessions engaging and super interactive. Delivery is everything.

Almost 10 Years as a Medical Doctor

As a fully qualified GP working in the NHS and private sector, Dr Sandhu has years of experience working with professionals with work-related stress, burnout, and many other mental and physical health conditions. He has further training in lifestyle medicine.

Lived Experience

From pursuing academic and sporting excellence at one of the world's top universities to long junior doctor night shifts, Dr Sandhu knows pressure. He also knows what it's like to experience burnout, recover from it, and thrive in high-stress environments.

Personalised to Your Workplace
